Favorite Places

Some of my favorite places to go.

This is where my wife and I were married. I love staying at the Ocean Beach Hotel that is only feet from the ocean!

There are amazing tide pools and the sunset cliffs are stunning!

Even tho I was raised in Utah I had never been to this waterfall before I met my wife! It is her favorite place in Utah and I love being able to take her there when I can.
